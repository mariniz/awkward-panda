Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. I also enjoy cooking eggs with tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  • 1 tomato

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted, add the eggs and cream mixture.
  4. Use a spatula to fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds. Add sliced tomatoes.
  5. Remove from heat while the eggs are still moist.
